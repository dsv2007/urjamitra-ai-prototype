# Pitch Script

## 30-Second Pitch

UrjaMitra AI turns existing MSME machines into intelligent energy-saving assets. Many MSMEs lose money through idle power, overheating, vibration and sudden breakdowns, but cannot afford to replace machines. Our low-cost IoT kit and AI Energy Twin dashboard monitor power, temperature, vibration, health, failure risk, carbon impact and savings in real time. It tells the owner what to switch off, what to repair and how much money can be saved. This is affordable Industry 4.0 for real MSMEs.

## 1-Minute Pitch

MSMEs often run old or semi-automatic machines without live energy monitoring or machine health tracking. This causes high bills, idle wastage, overheating, vibration issues and unplanned downtime. UrjaMitra AI solves this with a low-cost sensor kit and an AI Energy Twin for each machine. The prototype simulates live voltage, current, power, temperature, vibration, load, runtime and output. Our AI engine detects overheating, high vibration, high power with low output and long idle runtime. It then generates maintenance alerts, savings recommendations, ROI and green impact reports. The solution is practical, scalable and commercially viable through IoT kit installation, subscription dashboards, maintenance support and energy audit services.

## 3-Minute Pitch

The pain point is simple: MSME owners pay for wasted energy and breakdowns they cannot see. A packaging machine may be vibrating abnormally, a compressor may be running idle, and an injection moulding machine may be consuming high power at low output. Today these issues become visible only after the electricity bill rises or the machine fails.

UrjaMitra AI creates an affordable modernization layer for these machines. Instead of replacing machinery, we attach sensors for voltage, current, temperature and vibration, connect them through an ESP32 controller, and build an AI Energy Twin in software. The dashboard shows live energy, health score, failure risk, idle wastage, carbon impact, maintenance urgency and monthly saving potential.

The prototype is working with simulated IoT data for seven MSME machines. The AI engine is rule-based for explainability: high temperature triggers overheating warning, high vibration triggers maintenance alert, high power with low output triggers energy wastage detection, and heat plus vibration creates critical failure risk. The system also gives simple owner-friendly recommendations with expected saving, difficulty and priority.

Commercially, UrjaMitra AI can be sold as one-time IoT kit installation plus monthly dashboard subscription. Additional revenue can come from maintenance support, energy audit reports and sector-specific customization. It supports energy efficiency, green manufacturing, lower downtime and affordable Industry 4.0 adoption. With incubation support, this can become a deployable product for MSME clusters across India.

### Solution
- Low-cost IoT sensor kit plus AI Energy Twin dashboard.

### Architecture
- Machine sensors to ESP32 controller.
- MQTT/HTTP transmission to cloud or local gateway.
- Database stores readings.
- AI engine generates risk, alerts, recommendations and reports.
- Owner, technician and admin dashboards.

- Express API with AI rules.
- Seven sample MSME machines.
