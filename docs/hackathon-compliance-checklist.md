# MSME Idea Hackathon 6.0 Compliance Checklist

Source reviewed: `MSME_Idea_Hackathon_6.0_Instructions_for_Participation.pdf`

## Submission Window

- Portal: `https://innovative.msme.gov.in`
- Submission dates: 27 June 2026 to 14 July 2026
- Applicant must register on the portal.
- Applicant must choose one approved Host Institute before final submission.
- Host Institute choice and submitted information cannot be changed after submission.

## Eligibility And Document Readiness

- Participant must be an Indian citizen above 18 years of age.
- Student applicant must submit Student Identity Card valid for FY 2026-27.
- Government-issued proof of age is required, preferably Aadhaar.
- Idea must be submitted only once and to only one Host Institute.
- Duplicate or substantially similar multiple submissions can be disqualified.
- False information, misconduct or canvassing can lead to disqualification or blacklisting.

## Theme Fit

UrjaMitra AI primarily fits:

- Energy Efficiency: machine-level energy monitoring, low-cost energy solutions, measurable savings, retrofitting for existing MSMEs.
- Industry 4.0 and 5.0: IoT, digital twin, AI, smart production monitoring and predictive maintenance for modernization without replacing machinery.

Secondary relevance:

- Renewable Energy: green impact and energy optimization readiness.
- Other Frontier Technologies: AI and embedded electronics for MSME modernization.

## Mandate For Ideas

| Mandate | UrjaMitra AI Alignment |
|---|---|
| Ideation | AI Energy Twin concept for existing MSME machines |
| Marketing and Branding | Owner-friendly saving, risk and green impact messaging |
| Technology | IoT, AI rules, digital twin dashboard, APIs and database |
| Co-creation | Designed for MSME owner, technician and Host Institute mentor feedback |
| Social Innovation | Reduces cost and supports sustainable MSME operations |
| Entrepreneurship | Subscription plus IoT kit plus maintenance support model |
| Open Innovation | Can work with Host Institute, MSME clusters and sensor vendors |
| Business Model Innovation | Affordable retrofit modernization instead of machine replacement |

## Do's Covered

- Novel and innovative value.
- Useful to society and MSME ecosystem.
- Scalable across sectors.
- Reduces product/process cost through energy savings.
- Improves quality of life by reducing downtime and owner stress.
- Focuses on clean and green energy impact.

## Don'ts Covered

- Concept note and block diagram do not include personal name, phone number, email, college name or identity-revealing information.
- Block diagram and concept note do not include research paper references, newspaper references or individual/firm identity.
- The idea is not against established scientific principles.
- The applicant and mentor should remain different persons during actual submission.

## Evaluation Flow Awareness

1. Host Institute evaluation by expert panel.
2. Ministry-level document screening.
3. Domain Expert Selection Committee evaluation and scoring.
4. PMAC final approval.

## Funding And Incubation Fit

- Selected ideas can receive funding support up to Rs 15 lakhs.
- UrjaMitra AI has a clear use of funds: sensor prototype, embedded firmware, pilot installation, cloud/backend, dashboard hardening, field validation and commercialization.

## Final Submission Reminder

Before uploading, ensure the final portal entry is complete, coherent, non-duplicated and aligned with the selected Host Institute's capabilities. Incomplete or incoherent submissions are liable for rejection.
